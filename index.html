About AICA

About Amrit Indo Canadian Academy

Education is an important tool to strengthen and to promote the society which enables the country to have a healthy nation. For this purpose, educational temples pursuing the ethical values, headed by selfless and devoted personalities are needed.

S. Harminder Singh founded Amrit Educational Society in 2004. This society decided to establish Amrit Indo Canadian Academy and the Academy started functioning from 14.04.2005. The Academy has been affiliated with the CBSE New Delhi (Aff No.1630443).

Philosophy of AICA

"We cannot prepare the future for the youth but we can certainly prepare our youth for the future."

  • Build positive attitude, character & positive self-esteem
  • Build pleasing personality to be leaders of tomorrow
  • Become more responsible, emotionally stable and physically fit
  • Become intellectually vibrant, spiritually enlightened, socially committed and above all good human beings

School Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant learning community grounded in faith, gratitude, tolerance, reflection, and insight, delivered through accessible, high-quality education.

We are dedicated to developing well-rounded individuals using research-based, adaptive pedagogical models. Our teachings integrate the CBSE curriculum's core learning objectives with teachings from Shri Guru Granth Sahib ji, social-emotional learning, financial literacy, digital citizenship, essential study and communication skills.

Vision Statement

We envision a future where our students are honest, compassionate, resilient, and highly skilled global citizens, embodying the wisdom of Shri Guru Granth Sahib ji and equipped to navigate an ever-changing world.

We aspire to be a pioneering educational institution renowned for its innovative pedagogy, impactful community outreach, empowered educators and unwavering commitment to ethical leadership and lifelong learning.

Infrastructure

The Academy is situated on the periphery of Ludhiana which has very calm atmosphere free from smog-congested streets of the city. Its land measure about 8 acres. On the whole it is a unique gift of nature to the students and the staff to have a wordsworthian environment to pursue their goals.

The building of the Academy, which has picturesque surroundings, presents a rare architectural grandeur. It is centrally air conditioned. The building has two blocks — administrative and teaching. This ensures undisturbed studies of the students and help the administration to solve the problems of parents as well as that of teachers.

Academics

Curriculum & Admissions

Admissions

A child of 3 years age is admitted to Nursery and 4 years old to L.K.G. and so on. Admission to 1st class and above is subject to clearing a written test and to availability of seats. There is no test for admission to Kindergarten classes.

Documents Required:

  • Photo copy of Date of Birth certificate (attested)
  • School leaving certificate
  • Report card of class passed
  • Two latest passport size photographs
  • Latest photograph of family
  • Photocopy of Aadhar card of parents
  • Blood group report of student

Curriculum

The CBSE course curriculum is observed throughout. English and Punjabi are taught from Nursery onwards. Hindi is taught from Class III onwards as per the three language formula laid down by the board.

The NCERT books have been prescribed for all Secondary and Senior Secondary classes. Equal stress is laid on theory and practical education. Remedial therapy classes for those who need extra guidance are conducted in the school.
